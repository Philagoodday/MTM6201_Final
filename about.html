About me

Hello! My name is Chuhong Feng, and I am an artist and creative professional based in Ottawa. With a background in Film Production and Cultural Industry Management from Sichuan Film and Television University, I bring a rich blend of artistic vision and technical expertise to my work.

I specialize in commercial photography, utilizing my skills in Adobe software like Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and After Effects to create stunning visuals. My passion for storytelling extends to 3D modeling and animation, where I harness tools like 3ds Max and Cinema 4D to bring concepts to life. Currently, I am expanding my horizons by studying Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College. This program allows me to explore new media and integrate innovative design techniques into my projects. As the founder of Feng Photography, I am dedicated to capturing moments and creating compelling narratives through my lens. Whether it's through my second-hand camera business or my web development work for O4U Boutique, my goal is to blend creativity with technical skill to produce work that resonates and inspires.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my journey and work. I look forward to connecting with fellow creatives and exploring new opportunities in the world of art and design.
